Notes

  • These benchmarks were conducted with data split into 4 segments, utilizing an ASUS ZenBook 13 laptop with 4 physical CPUs and 4 hyperthreads.
  • Observed latencies include noise due to shared resources on the laptop; performance is expected to improve significantly on dedicated servers.
  • Queries were configured with a result limit of 300 properties, each covering 7 days, resulting in a response size of 412 KB. For typical user searches requesting 1 to 3 days, latencies are lower, and responses are faster.
  • Performance can be further enhanced by using more powerful hardware with additional CPUs and increasing the number of data segments.
