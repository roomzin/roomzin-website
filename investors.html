Market Opportunity

The global travel industry handles billions of queries per day. Online Travel Agencies (OTAs), hotel chains, and aggregators spend millions scaling infrastructure to maintain speed and reliability. Current solutions are:

  • Generic databases or caching layers not optimized for travel inventory
  • High-cost infrastructure with unpredictable latency under peak load
  • Complex setups requiring heavy engineering resources

Roomzin replaces these layers with a purpose-built, high-performance in-memory engine.

Why Roomzin Wins

  • Purpose-built for travel — understands segments, properties, room types, and date ranges natively.
  • Benchmark-proven — 10,000 requests/sec with median latency under 3ms on modest hardware.
  • Cost-efficient — compact memory footprint and small snapshot sizes reduce server costs.
  • Developer-friendly — one-query model removes complexity from integration.

Traction Potential

Roomzin appeals to OTAs, hotel chains, aggregators, and SaaS providers who face enormous pressure to deliver faster searches while reducing costs. The product is SaaS-ready and can also be licensed as enterprise software.
